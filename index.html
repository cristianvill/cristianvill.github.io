I'm a PhD student and DOE Computational Science Graduate Fellow working on multi-fidelity neural networks and uncertainty quantification. My research focuses on developing data-driven methods that efficiently combine information from multiple sources of varying accuracy and cost.

I'm interested in scientific machine learning, surrogate modeling, and how we can build better predictive models for expensive computational simulations. When I'm not debugging python scripts, you'll find me in the kitchen; turns out I approach both recipes and research the same way.
